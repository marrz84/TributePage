Stephen Robert Irwin

Steve Irwin, known as the "Crocodile Hunter", was an Australian zookeeper, conservationist, television personality, wildlife educator, and envirolmentalist

Steve Irwin holding a young gator, showing his excitment to be around wildlife.

Here's a timeline of Steve Irwin's life:

  • 1962 - Born on Febuary 22nd to Lynette Hakainsson and Bob Irwin
  • 1968 - Steve was given his first pet, a scrub python, igniting his passion for wildlife care
  • 1970 - Steve and his family moved from the suburbs of Melborne to Queensland where his parents started the Queensland Reptile and Fauna park
  • 1971 - Steve began helping around the park, handling crocodiles, and even wrestled his first one (under his father's supervision)
  • 1991 - Irwin took over the management of the park
  • 1991 - Met his soon-to-be wife Terri Raines
  • 1992 - Married Terri on June 4th
  • 1996 - The first episode of the Crocodile Hunter debuted. This show snowballed Irwin into starring in various other shows throughout the years
  • 1998 - Renamed the park the Australia Zoo
  • 1998 - Bindi Irwin, Steve's daughter was bron
  • 2003 - Robert Irwin, Steve's son was born
  • 2006 - Steve Irwin passed away due to a stingray puncturing his lung and the venom reaching his heart
